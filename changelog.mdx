<Update label="July 18, 2026" tags={["Live meetings"]}>
  ## Live meetings

  Meet with patients over secure video, straight from the browser — no
  downloads and no patient account required.

  - Start a meeting from your meeting room and invite a patient with a
    single invite link.
  - Talk over video or use screen sharing and text chat during the
    appointment.
  - Open the patient chart beside the call to keep clinical context in view.
  - Running an in-person appointment? Use audio-only mode to capture live
    audio without video.

  Read the [live meetings guide](/guides/live-meetings) to get started.
</Update>
