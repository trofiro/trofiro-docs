Quick answers to the questions practitioners and patients ask most. Can't find yours? Reach out at [support@trofiro.com](mailto:support@trofiro.com).

## About Trofiro

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Trofiro?">
    Trofiro is a web application for health practitioners. It runs entirely in the browser, and at its heart today is the **live meeting** — a secure video appointment where you talk with your patient over video, chat, share your screen, and keep the patient chart at your side.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything?">
    No. Trofiro runs in your browser on a computer or phone — there is nothing to download or install, for you or your patients.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I change the theme?">
    Select your name at the bottom of the sidebar to open your account menu, then choose **Theme** and pick **System**, **Light**, or **Dark**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I sign out?">
    Select your name at the bottom of the sidebar to open your account menu, then select **Sign out**.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Live meetings

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does my patient need a Trofiro account?">
    No. Patients join with the invite link you send them — no account, no password, no download. The link opens the join screen right in their browser.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does my patient need to join a meeting?">
    A browser on a computer or phone, a camera and microphone, and the invite link. The join screen lets them check both devices and enter their name before joining.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can a patient rejoin after leaving?">
    Yes. Opening the same invite link again rejoins the meeting, as long as the link hasn't expired.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long do invite links last?">
    Invite links expire after a set period. Send the link close to the appointment time — and if a patient sees "**Invalid or expired link**", copy a fresh one from the control bar and resend it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why did the meeting start as soon as I opened my room?">
    That's how meeting rooms work: opening the room connects you immediately, with camera and microphone on. There's no preview screen for practitioners, so check your setup before opening the room.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between leaving and ending a meeting?">
    When a patient selects the red end-call button, only they leave — the meeting continues. When the practitioner selects it, the meeting ends for everyone.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I use Trofiro live meetings on my phone?">
    Yes — meetings work in mobile browsers. On a phone, screen sharing isn't available, and the chat and patient chart overlay the video instead of docking beside it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is the connection private?">
    Yes — as the join screen puts it: "Your connection is private and encrypted."
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
