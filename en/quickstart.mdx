The fastest way to get to know Trofiro is to run a live meeting. In a few minutes you'll sign in, invite a patient, and finish your first appointment.

<Info>
  You need a Trofiro account and a desktop browser with a camera and microphone. Your patient doesn't need an account — only the invite link you send them.
</Info>

## Start your first meeting

<Steps>

  <Step title="Sign in and open your meeting room">
    Open Trofiro in your browser and select **Entrar** to sign in. After you authenticate, open your meeting room address — it looks like `…/live/your-room-name`.

    <Warning>
      You connect the moment the room opens — your camera and microphone are **on** right away, with no preview screen. Check your setup before you open the room.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite your patient">
    While you're alone in the room, select **Copy link** on the waiting card. You can also use the link button in the control bar at any time during the call.

    Send the invite link to your patient through any channel you already use.

    <Warning>
      Invite links expire after a set period. Send the link close to the appointment time, and copy a fresh one if the appointment was arranged long ago.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for your patient">
    The waiting card shows "**Waiting for \{name\} to join…**" along with the appointment time. You see your own camera while you wait.

    As soon as your patient joins, the meeting switches to the live view automatically.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run the appointment">
    Your controls sit in the bar at the bottom: microphone, camera, screen share, chat, and the patient chart. See [everything you can do during a meeting](/en/guides/live-meetings#host-a-live-meeting).
  </Step>

  <Step title="End the call">
    Select the red end-call button. The meeting ends for everyone — a loading page appears for a couple of seconds, then you're taken to your home page.
  </Step>

</Steps>

## What your patient sees

Your patient opens the invite link and lands on a "**Ready to join?**" screen. There they preview their camera, pick their microphone, enter their name, and select **Join room**. Read the full [patient journey](/en/guides/live-meetings#join-a-live-meeting-as-a-patient) so you can guide them if they get stuck.

  Want the complete picture — every control, the patient chart, audio-only mode, and more? Read the [live meetings guide](/en/guides/live-meetings).
</Tip>
