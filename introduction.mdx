Trofiro is a platform for practitioners who love their patients — one place to run your appointments, follow each patient's journey, stay in touch between visits, keep your finances in order, and stay on top of research, with an assistant at your side.

## Live meetings

At the heart of Trofiro today is the **live meeting**: a secure appointment with your patient — over video when you meet remotely, or audio-only when you're sitting together in person. Either way, the patient chart stays at your side for the whole appointment.
