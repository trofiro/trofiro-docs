## What can you do with Trofiro?

Trofiro brings together everything you need to work efficiently and at scale:

- **Manage your account and team** — Create an organization, invite members, and assign roles to control access across your workspace.
- **Navigate an intuitive dashboard** — Get a real-time overview of your resources, recent activity, and quick-action shortcuts all in one place.
- **Automate workflows** — Build powerful automation rules that trigger actions based on events, schedules, or conditions — no code required.
- **Connect third-party tools** — Integrate with your favorite apps and services through Trofiro's growing library of native integrations.
- **Stay informed with real-time notifications** — Receive instant alerts for the events that matter most, delivered in-app or to your preferred channel.
- **Customize your experience** — Tailor the dashboard layout, notification preferences, and workspace settings to match how you work.
- **Access the REST API** — Use Trofiro's fully documented API to build custom workflows, sync data, and extend the platform's capabilities.
- **Receive events via webhooks** — Subscribe to webhook events and let Trofiro push real-time updates directly to your systems.
