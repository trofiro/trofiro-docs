Trofiro is a web platform built for practitioners. At its heart is the **live meeting**: a secure video consultation between you and your patient that runs entirely in the browser. You host the meeting from your Trofiro account; your patient joins with a single invite link.

<Note>
  Everything happens in the browser — there is nothing to install, and your patients don't need a Trofiro account to join.
</Note>

## How a live meeting works

You open your meeting room and share a secure invite link with your patient. Your patient opens the link, checks their camera and microphone, enters their name, and joins the meeting. During the consultation you can talk over video, chat, share your screen, and keep the patient chart at your side. When you're done, you end the call for everyone.
