This guide walks you through the core building blocks of Trofiro. By the end, you'll have created your first resource, enriched it with tags and custom metadata, and learned how to search, filter, and manage your resources day-to-day.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your first resource">
    From the main navigation, go to **Dashboard > Resources** and click **New Resource**. A creation dialog appears with the following fields:

    - **Name** — a short, descriptive title for your resource.
    - **Description** — optional context about what the resource represents.

    Fill in the fields and click **Create**. Your new resource appears in the resource list immediately.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add tags">
    Open the resource you just created by clicking its name. In the detail panel, locate the **Tags** field and click it. Type a tag name and press **Enter** to apply it. You can add as many tags as you need — press **Enter** after each one. Tags are shared across your workspace, so any tag you create here becomes available to other resources.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add custom metadata">
    With the resource detail view open, click **Add Field** beneath the existing fields. Enter a **key** (the field name) and a **value**, then press **Enter** to save the pair. Repeat this process for each additional piece of metadata you want to capture. Custom fields are specific to the individual resource and do not affect other resources in your workspace.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update resource status">
    Click the **Status** dropdown near the top of the resource detail view. Choose from the available statuses:

    - **Active** — the resource is in use.
    - **Archived** — the resource is retained for reference but hidden from default views.

    The status updates immediately and is reflected in the resource list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Search and filter resources">
    Return to **Dashboard > Resources**. Use the **search bar** at the top of the list to search by resource name. To narrow results further, open the filter sidebar on the left and apply one or more filters:

    - **Tag** — show only resources with a specific tag.
    - **Status** — show only Active or Archived resources.

    Active filters are displayed as chips above the resource list. Click the **×** on any chip to remove that filter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Resource Actions

From the resource detail page, you can perform the following actions using the **Actions** menu (the `•••` button in the top-right corner of the detail panel):

- **Edit** — update the resource name, description, or any custom field.
- **Duplicate** — create an exact copy of the resource, including all tags and custom fields.
- **Archive** — move the resource out of active views while retaining its data.
- **Delete** — permanently remove the resource. This action cannot be undone.
- **Share** — generate a shareable link to the resource for teammates or external collaborators.

<Tip>
  To apply an action to several resources at once, select them using the **checkboxes** on the left side of each row in the resource list. Once two or more resources are selected, a bulk-action toolbar appears at the bottom of the screen. From there you can archive, delete, tag, or move all selected resources in a single operation.
</Tip>

To learn more about how resources are structured and what fields are available by default, see the [Data Model](/concepts/data-model) reference.
