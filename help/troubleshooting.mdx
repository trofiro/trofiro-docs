Something not working during a live meeting? Find the symptom below for the fix.

## Joining problems

### The invite link shows "Invalid or expired link"

Invite links expire after a set period for the patient's security.

- **Patients**: ask your practitioner to send you a new link.
- **Practitioners**: copy a fresh link from the **Copy link** button on the waiting card, or from the link button in the control bar, and resend it.

### My patient forgot or lost their link

Copy the link again and resend it — from the waiting card while you're alone in the room, or from the link button in the control bar during the call. The link stays the same while it's valid, so a fresh copy always works.

### "Name is required" when joining

The name field on the join screen can't be empty — it's how the patient appears in the call. Type a name, then select **Join room**.

## Camera and microphone

### The preview says "Your camera is off"

On the join screen, this appears when no video is coming through:

1. Turn the camera toggle on.
2. If it's already on, use the camera picker to select a different device.
3. Make sure your browser has permission to use the camera on this site — look for a camera icon in the address bar.

### Others can't hear me

1. Check that your microphone isn't muted — the microphone control shows its current state.
2. Use the microphone picker (desktop) to select the right device.
3. Make sure your browser has permission to use the microphone on this site.

## During the meeting

### There's no screen share button

Screen sharing is available on desktop browsers only. If you're on a phone or tablet, join from a computer to share your screen.

### My chat message won't send

Messages are limited to 750 characters — shorten the message and try again. Press **Enter** to send; **Shift+Enter** adds a new line instead of sending.

## Ending the call

### "Couldn't end the call for everyone. Please try again."

The request to end the meeting didn't go through. Select **End call** again — the button re-enables after the error.

### The meeting ended by itself

Your practitioner ended the call for everyone. Nothing is wrong — you'll see "**Your call has ended**", and you can safely close the window.
