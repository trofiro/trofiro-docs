Setting up your Trofiro account takes only a few minutes and gives you access to the full platform — including your personal workspace, team collaboration tools, and the Trofiro API. This guide walks you through every step, from registering for the first time to getting your whole team on board.

<Steps>

  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [app.trofiro.com](https://app.trofiro.com) and click **Sign Up**.

    Complete the registration form with your full name, email address, and a strong password. Then click **Create Account**.

    Trofiro creates your account and sends a verification email to the address you provided.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    Open the verification email from Trofiro (check your spam folder if it doesn't appear within a minute or two) and click **Verify Email Address**.

    You are redirected to Trofiro and your account is activated. Email verification is required before you can invite team members or generate API keys.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete your profile">
    After verification, Trofiro prompts you to complete your profile. Fill in the following fields:

    - **Full name** — How your name appears to teammates and in notifications
    - **Avatar** — Upload a profile photo (PNG or JPG, max 2 MB) so teammates can recognize you
    - **Timezone** — Select your local timezone so that scheduled workflows and notifications fire at the right time

    Click **Save Profile** to continue.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up your organization and workspace">
    Next, configure your organization — the top-level container for all your team's work in Trofiro.

    - **Organization name** — This is typically your company or team name (e.g., `Acme Corp`)
    - **Workspace name** — Your default workspace within the organization (e.g., `Product Team` or `Engineering`)

    Click **Create Organization** to finalize setup. You can create additional workspaces at any time from **Settings > Workspaces**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite team members">
    Bring your teammates into Trofiro so you can collaborate on resources and workflows together.

    1. Navigate to **Settings > Team**.
    2. Click **Invite Member**.
    3. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.
    4. Select a role for the new member (see the roles table below).
    5. Click **Send Invite**.

    The invitee receives an email with a link to join your organization. Pending invitations are listed under **Settings > Team > Pending Invites**, where you can resend or revoke them at any time.
  </Step>

</Steps>

## Account roles and permissions

Trofiro uses a role-based access control model to help you manage what each team member can see and do within your organization.

| Role | Permissions |
|------|-------------|
| **Owner** | Full access to all settings, billing, and members. Can delete the organization. There is one Owner per organization; ownership can be transferred. |
| **Admin** | Can manage team members, integrations, API keys, and workspace settings. Cannot access billing or transfer ownership. |
| **Member** | Can view and interact with resources and workflows they have been granted access to. Cannot modify organization settings or manage other users. |

You can change a member's role at any time from **Settings > Team** by clicking the role badge next to their name.

<Note>
  Billing and subscription management are handled separately from team settings. To view your current plan, upgrade, or manage payment methods, navigate to **Settings > Billing**. Plan details, usage limits, and invoices are all available there.
</Note>
