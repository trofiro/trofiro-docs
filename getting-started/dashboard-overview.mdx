The Trofiro dashboard is your central hub for everything happening in your workspace. From here you can browse and manage your resources, monitor real-time activity across your team, trigger quick actions, and navigate to every corner of the platform. This guide introduces each major area of the dashboard so you can get oriented and start working efficiently right away.

## Sidebar navigation

The sidebar runs along the left edge of the dashboard and is your primary way to move through the platform. It contains the following top-level destinations:

- **Workspaces** — Switch between the workspaces in your organization or create a new one.
- **Resources** — Browse, create, and manage all the resources in your current workspace.
- **Integrations** — Connect and configure third-party tools and services.
- **Settings** — Access your organization settings, team management, API keys, billing, and customization options.

Click the **«** icon at the top of the sidebar to collapse it into icon-only mode, giving your content area more room. Click the same icon to expand it again. Your preference is saved automatically.

## Resources panel

The Resources panel occupies the main content area of the dashboard and displays all resources in your active workspace. From here you can:

- **View resources** — Click any resource row to open its detail view, where you can see its full configuration, linked workflows, and history.
- **Search resources** — Use the search bar at the top of the panel to find resources by name or description instantly.
- **Filter resources** — Click **Filters** to narrow the list by creation date, status, tags, or the team member who created a resource.
- **Sort resources** — Click any column header to sort the list ascending or descending by that field.

To create a new resource directly from the panel, click **New Resource** in the top-right corner.

## Activity feed

The Activity Feed appears in the right-hand panel of the dashboard and provides a live, chronological log of everything that has happened in your workspace. Each entry shows:

- **What changed** — A plain-language description of the event (e.g., *Resource "Campaign Data" was updated*)
- **Who made the change** — The team member or API key responsible
- **When it happened** — A timestamp displayed in your configured timezone

Use the Activity Feed to audit recent changes, troubleshoot unexpected behavior, or simply stay in sync with your teammates without needing to ask for status updates. Click any activity entry to jump directly to the affected resource or workflow.

## Quick actions

The Quick Actions toolbar sits at the top of the dashboard, just below the main navigation bar. It provides instant access to the three most common tasks:

- **Create** — Opens the New Resource dialog without navigating away from your current view.
- **Search** — Launches the global search overlay, which searches across resources, workflows, integrations, and settings simultaneously. You can also open it with the keyboard shortcut `Cmd+K` (Mac) or `Ctrl+K` (Windows/Linux).
- **Invite** — Opens the team invitation dialog so you can bring a new member into your organization in seconds.

<Tip>
  You can rearrange dashboard panels, adjust the sidebar width, and toggle the Activity Feed on or off to suit your workflow. Head to **Settings > Customization** to configure your preferred layout — changes are saved to your profile and persist across devices.
</Tip>
