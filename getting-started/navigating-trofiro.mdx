This page gets you oriented in Trofiro: how to sign in, what's in the sidebar, and where your account options live.

## Sign in

Open Trofiro in your browser and select **Sign in**. After you authenticate, you land on your home page with the sidebar on the left.

## The sidebar

The sidebar is how you move around the platform. It shows the areas of Trofiro:

- **Search**
- **Appointments**
- **Patients**
- **Communication**
- **Assistant**
- **Finance**
- **Research**

Today, live meetings are where the action happens — see the [live meetings guide](/guides/live-meetings) for everything you can do in a consultation.

<Note>
  If you open a page without content yet, you'll see "**Nothing here yet**" with a hint to use the side menu — that's expected.
</Note>

## Your account menu

Your name and your role label (for example **Nutritionist**) sit at the bottom of the sidebar. Select them to open the account menu:

- **Theme** — switch between **System**, **Light**, and **Dark**.
- **Sign out** — end your session.
