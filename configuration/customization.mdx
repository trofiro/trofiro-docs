Trofiro gives you a wide range of personalization options so your workspace feels at home for your team. From switching color themes and uploading your logo to rearranging your dashboard and creating shared views, every customization you make helps your team work faster and more consistently.

## Theme and Appearance

Trofiro supports three display themes that you can switch at any time without affecting other workspace members.

- **Light mode** — a bright, high-contrast interface suited for well-lit environments.
- **Dark mode** — a low-brightness interface that reduces eye strain in dim settings.
- **System default** — automatically matches the theme to your operating system's current appearance setting.

To change your theme, go to **Settings > Appearance > Theme** and select your preferred option. Your theme preference is account-scoped, meaning it applies only to your own session and does not affect how other members see the workspace.

## Custom Branding

<Info>
  Custom branding is available on the **Pro plan and above**. If you are on the Free plan, upgrade at **Settings > Billing** to unlock this feature.
</Info>

Custom branding lets you display your organization's logo and brand color in the Trofiro workspace header, making the product feel native to your company. This is especially useful when sharing the workspace with clients or external collaborators.

To configure custom branding:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Appearance settings">
    Navigate to **Settings > Appearance > Branding**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your workspace logo">
    Click **Upload Logo** and select an image file. Trofiro accepts PNG, JPG, or SVG files up to 2 MB. The recommended dimensions are 200 × 50 px for best display in the header.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your brand color">
    Click the color swatch next to **Brand Color** and enter your hex color code, or use the color picker to select a color. This color is applied to the workspace header and key accent elements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save Branding**. Your logo and brand color will appear immediately for all workspace members.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Layout

Your Trofiro dashboard is fully rearrangeable, so you can surface the information most relevant to your workflow front and center.

- **Rearrange panels** — click and hold any panel header, then drag it to a new position on the dashboard grid. Drop it into an empty slot or swap it with an existing panel.
- **Pin and unpin widgets** — hover over any widget and click the **pin icon** to keep it anchored in place, or click it again to unpin and allow rearranging.
- **Save layout preferences** — once you have the dashboard arranged the way you like it, click **Save Layout** in the top-right corner of the dashboard. Your layout is saved to your account and persists across sessions and devices.

Layout preferences are account-scoped: your personal layout does not change the dashboard for other workspace members.

## Saved Views

Saved views let you capture a specific combination of filters, sorting, and grouping on any resource list so you can return to that exact perspective in one click. You can keep views private or share them with the whole workspace.

To create a saved view:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apply your filters and sorting">
    Open a resource list and use the **Filter** and **Sort** controls in the toolbar to set up the exact view you want — for example, filtering by assignee, sorting by due date, and grouping by status.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Click "Save as View"'>
    Click the **Save as View** button in the toolbar. A dialog will appear prompting you to name your view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the view">
    Enter a clear, descriptive name so you and your teammates can identify it at a glance (e.g., "My Open Items" or "High-Priority This Week").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose visibility">
    Select **Private** to keep the view accessible only to you, or **Shared** to make it visible to all workspace members in their sidebar. Click **Save** to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Share a standardized view with your team to ensure everyone is looking at the same filtered, sorted resource list during standups or planning sessions. Navigate to a saved view, click the **share icon** next to its name, and set it to **Shared** — it will appear in every member's sidebar automatically.
</Tip>

Saved views can be edited or deleted at any time by clicking the **three-dot menu** next to the view name in the sidebar.

## Custom Fields

Custom fields let you extend Trofiro's default resource properties with additional data specific to your team's process. Add and manage custom fields at **Settings > Custom Fields**.

When creating a custom field, assign it a name, choose a field type, and optionally mark it as required. Custom fields are available on all resource types by default, but you can scope a field to specific resource types if needed.

| Field Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Text | Free-form single-line or multi-line text input. |
| Number | Numeric values; supports integers and decimals. |
| Date | A date picker that respects the workspace date format setting. |
| Select | A single-choice dropdown from a predefined list of options. |
| Multi-select | A multi-choice picker allowing one or more values from a predefined list. |
| Checkbox | A boolean true/false toggle. |
| URL | A validated URL field that renders as a clickable link in the interface. |

To add a new custom field, click **Add Custom Field**, fill in the field name and type, define any options (for Select and Multi-select types), and click **Save**. The field will immediately appear on the resource detail panel for all members.
